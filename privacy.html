Privacy Policy

PropFirm Guardian · Grandmaster Labs · Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PropFirm Guardian ("the App", "we", "us"), published by Grandmaster Labs, handles information when you use the App. We have designed PropFirm Guardian to keep the large majority of your data on your own device. Please read this policy together with our Terms of Service.

1. Who we are

PropFirm Guardian is a mobile application that helps proprietary-trading-firm ("prop firm") traders monitor account metrics, calculate position sizing, track trading rules, and receive drawdown-related alerts. The App is operated by Grandmaster Labs. You can contact us at any time at grandmasterlabs01@gmail.com.

2. Information stored on your device

Most of the information you enter into PropFirm Guardian is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us. This includes:

  • Firm profiles you create (account sizes, drawdown limits, profit targets, and related rule settings);
  • Calculator inputs and preferences (such as your default risk percentage and currency symbol);
  • Trading journal entries, analytics derived from them, and liquidity-level notes;
  • News-calendar entries you add manually;
  • App settings and preferences;
  • Whether you have accepted the in-app risk disclaimer.

This on-device data remains under your control. Using the "Reset all data" function in the App's settings deletes this locally stored information from your device. Uninstalling the App also removes locally stored data.

3. Live MT5 account monitoring (optional feature)

If you choose to use the live monitoring feature, the App connects to a MetaTrader 5 ("MT5") account through a companion Expert Advisor (EA) that you install on your own trading terminal, using a connection token generated in the App. When this feature is active, trading-account metrics — such as balance, equity, and drawdown figures — are transmitted from your MT5 terminal to our server (hosted on Railway) and relayed to your device so the App can display live values and evaluate alert conditions.

This feature is optional. If you do not generate a connection token and install the EA, no MT5 account data is sent to our server.

We use this account-metric data only to provide the monitoring and alerting functionality you have requested. We do not sell it, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Subscriptions and purchases

PropFirm Guardian offers optional paid subscriptions (Pro). Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing and managed through RevenueCat, our subscription-management provider. When you purchase or restore a subscription, RevenueCat and Google process the transaction and provide us with your subscription and entitlement status (for example, whether your Pro entitlement is active). We do not receive or store your full payment-card details; these are handled by Google Play.

RevenueCat may assign an anonymous app-user identifier to your installation in order to track entitlement status. You can review RevenueCat's privacy practices at revenuecat.com/privacy and Google Play's at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Free trial

The App offers a time-limited free trial. To administer the trial (including preventing repeated trials on the same installation), the App communicates with our server to record and check trial status associated with an anonymous identifier. This does not require your name or email address.

6. Notifications

With your permission, the App sends local notifications on your device — for example, drawdown and margin alerts while monitoring is active, and trial-status reminders. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Notification permission is requested through your operating system.

7. Information we do not collect

  • We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

8. Data retention

On-device data persists until you delete it (via "Reset all data") or uninstall the App. Live MT5 metric data transmitted through our server is used to provide the live feature and is retained only as needed to operate that functionality. Subscription status is retained by Google and RevenueCat according to their respective policies.

9. Children

PropFirm Guardian is intended for adults engaged in trading activity and is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information transmitted to and from our server, including encrypted connections. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your choices

  • You can delete locally stored data at any time using "Reset all data" in Settings, or by uninstalling the App.
  • You can choose not to use the live MT5 monitoring feature, in which case no account metrics are sent to our server.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play.
  • You can disable notifications in your device settings.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at grandmasterlabs01@gmail.com.

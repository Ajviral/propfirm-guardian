Data Deletion Request

PropFirm Guardian · Grandmaster Labs · Last updated: 24 June 2026

This page explains how users of PropFirm Guardian (published by Grandmaster Labs) can delete their data, and what data is deleted or retained. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. Data stored on your device

Most of your data in PropFirm Guardian — including firm profiles, trading journal entries, analytics, liquidity notes, calculator inputs, news-calendar entries, and app settings — is stored only on your own device. You can delete this data yourself at any time, with no request needed:

  • In the app: open Settings → Data Management → "Reset all data". This permanently erases all locally stored data from your device.
  • Uninstall: removing the app from your device also deletes all locally stored data.

2. Data held on our server

If you use the optional live monitoring feature, or while a free trial is active, a limited amount of data is held on our server. This consists of an anonymous identifier used to operate the trial and subscription functionality, and any live trading-account metrics relayed while the live feature is active. This data is not linked to your name or email address.

3. How to request deletion of server-held data

To request deletion of any data associated with your installation that is held on our server, email us:

Send an email to grandmasterlabs01@gmail.com with the subject line "PropFirm Guardian Data Deletion Request".

To help us locate and delete the correct data, please include in your email:

  1. A statement that you are requesting deletion of your data.
  2. If you used the live monitoring feature, the connection token(s) you generated in the app (found on the Live Connections screen), if available.

We will action verified deletion requests within 30 days of receipt.

4. What is deleted and what is kept

  • Deleted: the anonymous identifier associated with your installation and any live trading-account metrics held on our server in connection with your request.
  • Deleted by you directly: all on-device data, via "Reset all data" or by uninstalling the app (see Section 1).
  • Retained where required: subscription and purchase records held by Google Play and our subscription provider (RevenueCat) are governed by their respective policies and retention periods, and may be retained as required for billing, tax, or legal compliance. We do not separately store your payment details.

Aside from records required for the purposes above, server-held data is retained only as long as needed to operate the trial, subscription, and live monitoring functionality, and is deleted on verified request as described in Section 3.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about deleting your data, contact us at grandmasterlabs01@gmail.com.
